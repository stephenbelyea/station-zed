About the Show

Two friends (Keegan and Chris) rewatch every James Bond film and pair it with a delectable alcoholic beverage themed to the film they’re watching. They watch the movie, drink, and then things get sloppy.

Boozing & Bonding‘s main online home is over at boozingandbonding.com — check out the site for updates and info about the show.
